Who we are

Savera Foodss was established in the year 2014 and has created a remarkable niche in the market. Our company is a partnership based firm. Operational headquarter of our company is situated at Pollachi, Tamil Nadu (India). We are the engaged in manufacturing and trading of Fruit Drink, Soft Drink and many more. These products are safe to consume.Our infrastructure comprises of departments such as:

  • Sales department
  • Quality department
  • Administrative department
